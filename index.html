Musical innovation or degradation?

Sampling - today one of the most popular and powerful tools in music production at first interpreted simply as stealing. Nowadays consider as a way to push a new life into the old classics. Invented in the middle 70's now at it's pick of popularity: used wordwide by artist representing almost every possible genre. Method of sampling involves "borrowing" a section of audio track- and then reinventing, panipulating and changing for your own music purpose. Some musicans are still strongly convinced it's only a shameless and easy way of plagiarism, others that it's a form of art that requires high level of creativity and developed imagination or forward thinking.
